Want an easy way to buy or sell textbooks?
I want to buy!
Step 1: Pick a book
Plug in your course name and lecture number and all you have to do is pick out of a list of the books.
Step 2: Pick a friend
Now you have to select which friend you'll buy it from after a list is given to you.
Step 3: Pick a place
Specify a location and time at which the book transaction will be made.
I want to sell!
Step 1: Pick a book
Either input the book's ISBN or choose a course and lecture to pick a book.
Step 2: Pick your conditions
Decide on a price and general time/location to sell your book.
